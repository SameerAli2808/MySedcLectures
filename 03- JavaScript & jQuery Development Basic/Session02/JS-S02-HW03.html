JavaScipt - Session 02 - Bonus Homework

Make a crystal ball program that predicts the future.

The program generates a random number from 1 to 5

The crystal ball should give you a response:

1 - You are on the right track.

2 - Danger is near.

3 - Luck will find you.

4 - You are about to make a mistake.

5 - Your future is unclear.
